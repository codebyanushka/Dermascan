What is this?
A skin reaction caused by direct contact with an irritant or allergen. Your skin is reacting to something it touched — like soap, a plant, or fabric.
⚠️
Is it serious?
Usually not serious. Most cases clear up on their own in 2–4 weeks once you avoid the trigger. But if it spreads or gets worse, see a dermatologist.
🔎
Likely Causes
Allergic reaction Harsh soap/detergent Plant contact Synthetic fabric
😣
Symptoms
Redness & inflammation Itching/burning Dry patches Skin tenderness

Home Remedies
Cool compress to reduce inflammation · Aloe vera gel · Colloidal oatmeal bath · Avoid scratching
